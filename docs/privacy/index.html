Privacy Policy

Within is designed for private reflection, wellness practice, learning, nutrition support, and peer-support features. This policy explains what the app stores and how to contact us about privacy.

Information you provide

Depending on the features you use, Within may ask for profile details, focus preferences, journal entries, sleep notes, dream notes, nutrition logs, hydration records, community posts, reports, blocks, friend requests, support requests, and subscription status.

Device-protected records

The native iPhone app is built so sensitive personal records such as local journals, sleep records, dreams, nutrition logs, private progress photos, profile snapshots, and app preferences stay protected on your device when those features are local. The app uses iOS storage protections and Keychain-backed encryption where implemented.

Server features

Some features require a server, such as AI guide requests, community rooms, reports, support requests, billing-related entitlement checks, referrals, and account deletion requests. Do not send passwords, payment-card numbers, emergency details, or medical records through support forms or community messages.

AI guide

When live AI is enabled, guide messages may be sent to Within's server and then to the configured AI provider so the guide can respond. Within should not store API keys in the iPhone app. The AI guide is not a clinician and does not diagnose, treat, or replace emergency care.

Community safety

Community messages may be filtered, reported, blocked, reviewed, removed, or retained long enough for moderation and safety review. Members should not post contact details, threats, encouragement of harm, medical instructions, or private information about another person.

Subscriptions

Purchases are handled by Apple. Within does not receive or store your full payment-card number. Apple may provide purchase status needed to unlock subscription features. Manage cancellation, renewals, and billing through your Apple account.

Children

Within is intended for adults. Do not use Within if you are under 18.

Deletion

You can erase local device data from the app's Account area where available. To request deletion of server-side account records, use the account deletion page or email support@withinai.app.

Contact

For privacy questions, email support@withinai.app or use the support page.
