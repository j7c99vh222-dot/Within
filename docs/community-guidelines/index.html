Community Standards

Within community rooms are for small, time-limited, supportive peer discussion. They are not therapy, emergency response, medical advice, dating, promotion, or private messaging for off-platform contact.

Post support, not instructions for harm

Encourage grounding, help-seeking, rest, reflection, and professional support. Do not encourage self-harm, relapse, restriction, purging, violence, stalking, harassment, or unsafe medical behavior.

Protect privacy

Do not post phone numbers, email addresses, home addresses, social handles, payment details, medical records, or identifying information about another person.

No harassment or hate

Do not attack, threaten, shame, sexualize, exploit, impersonate, or target people based on protected traits, health, recovery status, body size, religion, culture, disability, or identity.

Reports and blocking

Use in-app reporting when content feels unsafe or violates these rules. Blocking is immediate where available. Report snapshots may be retained for review and member-visible moderation responses.

Moderation actions

Within may remove messages, restrict access, preserve report details, respond to reporters, or escalate urgent safety concerns where legally required or appropriate for user safety.

Contact

For community moderation questions, email support@withinai.app or use support.
