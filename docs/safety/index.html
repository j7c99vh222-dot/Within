Safety

When you need more than the app.

Within can support reflection and coping, but it is not emergency care, therapy, medical treatment, detox support, or a substitute for a qualified professional.

Immediate danger: Call emergency services now. In the United States, call or text 988 for suicide and crisis support.

Use professional or emergency help for

  • Thoughts of self-harm, suicide, violence, or immediate danger.
  • Overdose risk, withdrawal symptoms, or substance-use emergencies.
  • Chest pain, fainting, severe pain, rapid unexplained weight change, or other urgent medical symptoms.
  • Eating-disorder behaviors, purging, severe restriction, or medical instability.
  • Medication questions, diagnosis, treatment planning, or crisis decisions.
