Account deletion

Delete Within account data.

Within has both local device data and server-side records, depending on which features you use. Follow both steps when you want a complete deletion request.

Erase local iPhone data

  1. Open Within on your iPhone.
  2. Go to More, then Account and settings.
  3. Choose Erase all local Within data.
  4. Confirm the warning. This removes local profile data, journal records, dreams, sleep records, food history, and private progress photos stored by the native app on that device.

Request server-side deletion

Email support@withinai.app with the subject "Within account deletion request" from the email address connected to your account. Include your Within username if you have one.

Subscriptions

Deleting app data does not cancel Apple billing. Manage subscription cancellation through Apple subscriptions.

Retention

Some records may be retained where required for legal, security, billing, fraud-prevention, moderation, or safety reasons. We will confirm what can be deleted after verifying the request.
