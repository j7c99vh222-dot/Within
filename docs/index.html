Help for the app, your account, and your safety.

What we can help with

Use this page for app support, App Store review links, creator program questions, financial access requests, privacy requests, and moderation concerns.

Subscriptions

For purchases, cancellations, restorations, and billing questions, manage Apple subscriptions in your Apple account first. We can help explain what the app is showing.

Privacy

Journals, sleep records, dreams, nutrition logs, and private progress photos are designed to stay protected. See the privacy page for details and deletion paths.

Community

Report unsafe content in the app when possible. We review reports, blocks, moderation concerns, and requests about community standards.

Accessibility

Send issues with text size, contrast, navigation, screen reader labels, or motion. Include your iPhone model and iOS version if you know them.

Contact

Reach human support

This form opens your email app with the details filled in. Do not include passwords, payment-card numbers, medical records, or emergency information.

For immediate danger or thoughts of self-harm, call emergency services or contact 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline in the United States.

You can also email support@withinai.app.
