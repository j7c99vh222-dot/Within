WITHIN IPHONE APP

OPEN THIS FILE IN XCODE:
Within.xcodeproj

1. Open Xcode.
2. Choose "Open Existing Project..." or File > Open.
3. Open Within.xcodeproj from this folder.
4. Click the blue Within project icon in the left sidebar.
5. Open Signing & Capabilities and choose your Apple Developer Team.
6. Choose an iPhone simulator at the top of Xcode.
7. Press the triangular Run button.

Do not select an individual Swift file and do not select the Within source folder.
